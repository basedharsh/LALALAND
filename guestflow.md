# Guest Flow

The Guest Flow in the Flutter Firebase Goodies Shopping App allows users to explore the app without logging in. Guests can browse products, view product details, and access limited features.

## Features Available to Guests

- **Browse Products**: Guests can view the list of available products.
- **Product Details**: Guests can click on a product to see detailed information.
- **Limited Access**: Certain features, such as adding products to the cart or making purchases, are restricted to logged-in users.
